About Us

Advanced Positive Change of Nigeria (APCON) is a group of progressive minded forward looking vibrant professionals in politics across the length and breadth of Nigeria

Vision

To be the foremost Lobbyist for Inclusive Development and Good Governance in Nigeria.

Mission

To work assiduously with Government at all levels to ensure that dividends of democracy reach the electorates and indeed the Nigerian Citizenry.

Monitoring

To monitor and gather intelligence reports on the level of impacts of Government policies on the welfare of the people for maximum and equitable distribution of dividends of democracy.

Good Governance

To advocate for the promotion of good governance and inculcate its values in the psyche of Nigerian political elites.

Fight against CIIDI

To be vanguard against all manifestations of Corruption, Inequality, Injustice, Discrimination and Insecurity in the country.

Support/Promote

To support and promote the aspirations for elective Government offices of credible, patriotic and integrity Candidates who are on the same page with us in our VISION and MISSION, during Nigeria's general elections.
